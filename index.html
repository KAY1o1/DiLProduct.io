A Day In A Life

Jason usually have various times of waking up. On this this day pov, he was up late and woke up around 11.

He starts his day by taking a shower and brushing his teeth too, to be more efficient. While the shower heats up, he fetches a change of clothes, so when he finish showering, it'll be easier to get dressed. After a shower, he packs his bag and, if he has time, has breakfast.

For his commute, he pays for parking near the station and has to transfer. During this time, he listened to audiobooks to pass the time. When he reached Jamaica, he met with a friend to catch the 6 train.

When he gets to campus, he goes to Gong Cha for a treat and prepares for his first class at 2 pm. The class usually ends at 5:50 pm, but it ran late due to group project presentations.

His Last class ended around 7 pm, but there was an event, the department production, that he remembered he had at 7. Fortunately, he was able to make it on time to see the show and had to rush to catch the 9:12 pm train at Grand Central.

When he got off the train, he took his car to the shop, and a friend picked him up to drop him home. At 11:30 pm, he was able to get a chance to eat, got Wendy's, after a long day. And to wind down, he takes a show and goes to bed.
